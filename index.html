Cybersecurity Awareness
🔒
Always check HTTPS
Legitimate sites use HTTPS. If you see HTTP (no 'S'), your data is unencrypted.
🔍
Verify the domain carefully
Attackers use lookalike domains like "paypa1.com". Always double-check spelling.
⚡
Beware of urgency tactics
Phishing sites create false urgency. Legitimate orgs don't pressure you to act immediately.
🔗
Avoid URL shorteners
Short URLs hide the real destination. Use tools to expand and check them first.
🎣
Spot phishing keywords
URLs with "login" or "verify" in unusual domains are red flags.
📧
Email link safety
Never click links in suspicious emails. Navigate to official websites manually.
